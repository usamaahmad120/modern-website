(About me)

I am a product and motion designer who loves creating things that are not just visually appealing but also solve everyday problems. When I’m not working on my computer or sketching on my iPad, you might find me leveling up in video games, capturing moments with my camera, or watching football because, for me, football is life. My design philosophy is simple: keep it stylish, make it functional, and add a touch of fun. I’m the designer you want on your team if you want people to say, "I need that in my life!"
